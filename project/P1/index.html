The Joy of Indoor Gardening

Why Keep Houseplants?

Houseplants do more than just look good. They can boost your mood, improve air quality, and help you focus.

Essential Tools for Beginners

You do not need a lot of equipment to start. Here are the three most important items:

  • Watering Can: A vessel with a thin spout helps reach the soil directly.
  • Pruning Shears: Sharp scissors to trim dead leaves and encourage growth.
  • Potting Mix: Specialized soil that provides drainage and nutrients.

Common Easy-to-Grow Plants

If you are worried about keeping plants alive, start with a Snake Plant. They are incredibly resilient.
